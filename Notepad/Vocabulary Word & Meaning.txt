Vocabulary Word & Meaning
jargon : special or technical words that are used by a particular group of people in a particular profession and that other people do not understand
Syntactic sugar : syntax that makes code easier to read and write without changing its behaviour.
Pathetic : causing you to feel pity or sadness / very bad, weak or useless Ex : Pathetic experience {Bad experience}
fragile : easily damaged or broken Ex: Their fragile friendship could not survive the misunderstanding.
